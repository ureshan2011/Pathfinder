Cookie & Local Storage Policy

Effective: 22 June 2026 Last updated: 22 June 2026 Version: 1.0

PathFinder is built to be light on tracking. We use no advertising cookies and no third-party analytics trackers. We do use your browser's local storage to remember your planning data and preferences, and a small number of strictly necessary cookies are set by the services we rely on (such as sign-in). This page explains what is stored and how you can control it.

01 What these technologies are

Cookies are small text files a website can store in your browser. Local storage (and similar web-storage APIs) lets a site save larger amounts of data in your browser that persists between visits. PathFinder relies mainly on local storage rather than cookies, because it lets the app work fast and offline while keeping your planning data on your own device.

02 How PathFinder uses them

  • Remember your work — your assessment answers, roadmap, saved items, application tracker, visa checklist, settlement scenarios, and drafts are cached locally so they load instantly and survive a page refresh.
  • Keep you signed in — if you sign in, our authentication provider stores a session so you don't have to log in on every visit.
  • Preferences — small settings such as currency and display choices.

We do not use cookies or storage for advertising, cross-site tracking, or selling your data.

04 Third-party cookies

Some essential third parties we use may set their own cookies when their features load:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and security may set session/token cookies or storage entries when you sign in.
  • PayHere — sets cookies during checkout if you make a payment; governed by PayHere's own policy.
  • PayPal — sets cookies on its hosted checkout if you pay via PayPal; governed by PayPal's own policy.
  • Fonts & CDNs — serving fonts or libraries may involve standard request data, but we do not embed advertising trackers.

These are controlled by those providers under their own cookie and privacy policies.

05 Managing & clearing your data

You are in control:

  • In the app — much of your planning data can be edited, exported, or removed from within PathFinder.
  • Clear browser storage — use your browser's settings to clear site data/cookies for PathFinder. This removes local-only planning data on that device, so export anything you want to keep first.
  • Block cookies — your browser can block or limit cookies, though blocking strictly necessary ones may stop sign-in or some features from working.
  • Private/incognito mode — storage is cleared when you close the window.
devices Heads-up: clearing browser storage on a device removes any data that was only stored locally there. If you signed in and enabled cloud sync, your data can be restored on next sign-in; if you were anonymous, it cannot.

06 Changes to this policy

If we introduce new cookies or storage uses (for example, optional analytics), we will update this page and, where the law requires consent, ask for it before such cookies are set.
